### 1. Message Tone Calibration

The AI must adjust message tone based on the specific pairing, NOT just the recipient's sign.

- Message frequency: Moderate -- the relationship flows naturally; nudges keep momentum

**Medium-compatibility pairings (5-7):**
- Tone: Constructive, bridging, and translating between different love languages
- Message frequency: Higher -- these pairings need more active guidance
- Example calibration: For Leo Man x Cancer Woman (7/10), the AI translates his public nature for her private needs. "I know I love the spotlight, but you are my favorite place to come home to."

**Low-compatibility pairings (3-4):**
- Tone: Empathetic, coaching, and gap-bridging. Focus on what IS working rather than what is not
- Message frequency: Highest -- these pairings need constant support and reframing
- Example calibration: For Sagittarius Man x Cancer Woman (3/10), the AI provides reassurance scripts and freedom-with-safety frameworks. "I am thinking about you from [location]. I will be home [specific day]. You are my safe place."

### 2. Gift Recommendation Engine

The AI should cross-reference:
- **His sign's natural giving style** (e.g., Leo = grand gestures, Virgo = practical quality)
- **Her sign's receiving preferences** (e.g., Cancer = sentimental, Aquarius = unique/cause-driven)
- **The pairing's gift strategy** (specific bridge recommendations from this document)

**Algorithm Logic:**
```
IF his_giving_style MATCHES her_receiving_preference:
     Recommend gifts aligned with both (e.g., Taurus Man x Taurus Woman = luxury quality)
ELIF his_giving_style CONFLICTS with her_receiving_preference:
     Use the "bridge gift" strategy from the pairing guide
     Example: Leo Man x Virgo Woman = "Add meaning to luxury" -- a premium wellness item instead of a flashy display
```

**Budget Sensitivity by Pairing:**
- Earth-heavy pairings (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn): Higher budgets expected; quality matters
- Fire-heavy pairings (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius): Experience over price; boldness matters
- Air-heavy pairings (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius): Cleverness over cost; novelty matters
- Water-heavy pairings (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces): Sentiment over spend; emotional depth matters

**For medium-compatibility pairings:**

**For low-compatibility pairings:**
- "Preventive" cards that anticipate friction points before they escalate
- Higher urgency, detailed coaching
- Example: "You have been traveling for 3 days. Her sign needs connection. Call her tonight -- even 5 minutes prevents the spiral."
