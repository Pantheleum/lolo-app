## 8. Malay Market Influencers

### TikTok-First Strategy for Malaysia

**Why TikTok First:**
- Malaysia has 20M+ TikTok users (62% of internet users)
- Malay-language content has strong viral potential
- TikTok Shop integration enables direct conversion
- Young Malaysian men (22-35) are highly active on TikTok
- Cost per impression is significantly lower than Instagram in Malaysia
- Relationship/couple content consistently trends on Malaysian TikTok

**TikTok Strategy Framework:**

| Element | Strategy |
|---------|----------|
| **Content Format** | 30-60 second Reels, "storytime" format, "POV" format, duets |

| **Hashtags** | #LOLO #WingmanRahsia #HubunganPower #CoupleGoalsMY #LelakiMalaysia |
| **Sound Strategy** | Use trending Malaysian sounds and music |

- Instagram (Reels + Stories) for 25-45 demographic
